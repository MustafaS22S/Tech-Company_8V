KY-015 DHT 11 Temperature Sensor

150.00 EGP

The KY-015 is a temperature and humidity sensor module that typically uses the DHT11 or DHT22 sensor. It measures temperature ranging from 0-50°C (DHT11) or -40 to 80°C (DHT22) and humidity from 20% to 80% RH (DHT11) or 0% to 100% RH (DHT22). The module communicates with microcontrollers using a single digital pin, making it easy to integrate into various electronics projects. It operates at 3.3V or 5V DC and is commonly used in weather stations and home automation systems.
